About

Cloud 6's goal is to give refugees of the world another chance after they have lost their previous home because of conflict in their country.

  • Use Tello Drones to give supply drops for refugees.
  • Use split off organinzations to raise money as funds for refugees.
  • Hold charity events/concerts to raise money.
  • Use Tello Drones to survey areas to find refugees and perform rescue operations.

Because of the Tello Drones finding and helping refugees wouldn't be a challenge. These drones are about 80g in weight built in Range Finder, WIFI 802.11n 2.4G, an HD 720P 30 live view camera, and battery that lasts up to 13min.
